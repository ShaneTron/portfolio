About Me

I grew us in an Airforce family so we traveled a lot throughout my younger years. I feel this made my a stronger person since I was always having to adjust to new environments every two or three years. Although I was not born or spend much of my youth in Washigton. I do consider myself from the area. Both my mother and father were born in the PNW and most of my extended family lives in the area. I have lived in the city of Seattle for over ten year. Previous to living in the City, I lived 45 minutes south in the city of Lakewood. I spent much of my younger years there. I first moved to Seattle to attend culinary school.

My Work History

From a young age I always dreamed of being a chef. I started out in kitchens and the service industry at an early age. From day one I was hooked. After working my teen aged years in Lakewood at several restaurants and coffee shops around town in both FOH and BOH positions I decided to move to the big city. I have been in the City for twelve years now and have spent the whole time chacing the dream of becoming a chef and opening my own restaurant.
